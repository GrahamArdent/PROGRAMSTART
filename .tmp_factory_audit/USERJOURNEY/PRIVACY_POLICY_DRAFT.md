## Privacy Policy

### 1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when it may be processed by service providers, and what choices you may have.

### 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

#### Account Information

- email address
- authentication identifiers
- account status and login events

#### Resume And Profile Information

- resume content you upload or enter
- employment history, education, skills, summary text, contact fields, and related profile data
- saved resume versions and exported content metadata

#### Job Description And Tailoring Information

- job descriptions you paste or upload
- target roles, target companies, and tailoring preferences

#### Generated And Derived Information

- AI-assisted suggestions
- rewritten bullets, summaries, recommendations, analyses, and tailored drafts
- preference settings and workflow state related to your use of the product

#### Technical And Usage Information

- session data
- device or browser information
- logs, diagnostics, crash information, and operational telemetry

### 3. How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

1. create and manage accounts
2. authenticate users and secure the service
3. store resumes, profiles, job descriptions, and related materials
4. analyze, improve, tailor, and export resume content
5. power AI-assisted features and workflow recommendations
6. maintain, troubleshoot, secure, and improve the service
7. detect fraud, abuse, misuse, or policy violations
8. communicate service-related notices and updates

### 4. AI And Automated Processing

Some features may process resume content, job descriptions, or related user-provided text with AI-powered or automated systems in order to analyze, improve, summarize, or tailor content.

As a result:

1. content you submit for those features may be sent to third-party AI or infrastructure providers involved in delivering the service
2. generated output may be stored with your account so you can review and continue working on it
3. automated output may be inaccurate, incomplete, or unsuitable for your intended use, and you are responsible for reviewing it before relying on it

If the service does not use customer content to train our own models, that fact should be stated explicitly in the final policy.

### 5. Sharing And Service Providers

We may share or make information available to service providers that help us operate the service, such as providers involved in:

1. authentication
2. database and cloud storage
3. hosting and infrastructure
4. logging, monitoring, and analytics
5. AI-assisted processing and content generation

At the time this draft was prepared, the product planning and repository evidence pointed to a stack centered on Supabase for authentication and persistence, with AI-assisted processing through configured model providers and related infrastructure where enabled. Final public disclosures should reflect the providers actually used in production.

We may also disclose information where required by law, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a business transfer as permitted by law.

### 6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain account functionality, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, protect the service, and preserve limited backup integrity.

Specific retention periods should be finalized based on actual operational practices before publication.

### 7. Your Choices And Rights

Depending on how the service is implemented and your jurisdiction, you may be able to:

1. access or update account information
2. edit or delete saved resume and profile content
3. export your content
4. request account deletion
5. manage optional communication preferences

The final policy should describe the actual deletion and export paths the product will support.

Until a self-serve account deletion path is implemented and verified, public policy text should avoid promising self-serve deletion.

### 8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect information. However, no method of transmission, storage, or processing can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

### 9. Children's Privacy

The service is not intended for children below the age permitted by applicable law for account creation and use, and we do not intend to knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law.

### 10. International Processing

Depending on where our providers and systems operate, information may be processed in jurisdictions different from your own. Final disclosure should be aligned with actual provider locations and legal obligations.

### 11. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may provide notice through the service, by email, or by another reasonable method.

### 12. Contact

Provide an official privacy or support contact for questions, requests, and complaints related to this Privacy Policy.
